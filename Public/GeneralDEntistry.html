General Dentistry

Our general dentistry services are designed to help you maintain optimal oral health and address any issues that may arise.

Pulp Test

The pulp test evaluates the health of the dental pulp to determine if a root canal is needed.

X-Ray

X-rays are used to diagnose dental issues such as cavities, infections, and bone loss that are not visible during a regular exam.

Oral Cancer Screening

Oral cancer screening involves examining the mouth and throat for signs of cancer or precancerous conditions.

Cavity Test

Cavity tests help in detecting tooth decay that may not yet be visible but could cause problems if left untreated.
